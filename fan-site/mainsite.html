What defines a weird image?

A weird image is not simply a weird image. If it has context, then it's not weird. If it's justifiable and explainable, than it's simply odd. But a weird image is absurd without explanation. If you can't describe what it means, it's truly strange.

Good attributes of a strange image

Here are some essential attributes a strange image should possess.

  • Somewhere unknown
  • No context
